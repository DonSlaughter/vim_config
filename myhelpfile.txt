Important shortcuts

split windows
	Ctrl + H,J,K,L moves split to position.

moving lines in visualmode
	select lines and move with J & K

g commands:
	g0 and g$ first and last character on broken line
	gq longline into multiple lines
	gu and gU Uncapitalize and capitalize text object
	~ and g~ switch capitalization of letter
	gf open highlighted text (file)
	gv reselect previous selected text
	g& rerun substitute command for all lines
